## What This Is

The Operations Command Center is a live map of the Phoenix Metro that shows every active job in JobTread, every lead in the GoHighLevel sales pipeline, all nine service zones, and every supplier location we use — all in one place. It refreshes overnight so it always reflects current data.

---

## Reading the Map

### Job & Lead Pins

| Symbol | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| ● Circle | Teal | JobTread — **Active Production** (job underway) |
| ● Circle | Blue-green | JobTread — **Sold / Project Intake** (sold, not yet active) |
| ◆ Diamond | Orange | GHL — **Appointment Booked** |
| ◆ Diamond | Yellow | GHL — **Consultation Completed** |
| ◆ Diamond | Bright Yellow | GHL — **Proposal Sent** |
| ◆ Diamond | Red | GHL — **Permitting** |

**Hover** over any GHL Appointment Booked pin to see the scheduled date and time.
**Click** any pin to open a popup with the full details — name, address, service zone, status, and a direct link to open that record in JobTread or GoHighLevel.

### Service Zones

The shaded polygons across the map represent our nine service zones (Z1 through Z5B). Every job and lead popup tells you which zone that address falls in.

---

## Filtering the Map

Use the **filter chips** along the top toolbar to show or hide specific categories:

- **JT ▾** — toggles all JobTread jobs on/off. Click individual chips (Active, Sold / Intake) to filter by status.
- **GHL ▾** — toggles all GHL pipeline leads on/off. Click individual chips to filter by stage.
- **Zone chips (Z1, Z2A, etc.)** — click any zone chip to show only pins within that zone.

---

## Using the Search Bar

Type any of the following into the search bar at the top of the map:

- **A supplier name** (e.g., "SiteOne", "Floor & Decor", "Aquatec") — the map flies to that pin and highlights it.
- **A job name or client name** — flies to that job or lead pin.
- **A street address** — geocodes the address, drops a pin, and tells you which service zone it falls in. Use this to quickly zone-check any address a customer or subcontractor gives you.

---

## Supplier Layers

The right-hand panel lists all supplier categories. Each is **OFF by default** to keep the map clean. Toggle any layer ON to show those locations as pins.

| Icon | Meaning |
|---|---|
| ★ Gold Star | **Showroom** — walk-in, client-facing, product displays |
| ◆ Diamond | **Supply / Distribution** — contractor pickup, not a showroom |
| 💧 Water Drop | **Pool & Fountain supplier** |

**All ON / All OFF** buttons at the top of the panel toggle every supplier layer at once.

---

## Weather Overlay

The **precipitation radar** is on by default. It shows live rain and storm coverage across the service area. Use it to check conditions before dispatching crews or scheduling outdoor work. Toggle it off using the **Radar ON/OFF** button at the bottom of the right panel if it's in the way.

---

## Dashboard

The **Live Dashboard** (upper left) shows a real-time count of jobs and leads by category:

- **Active Production** — jobs currently being built
- **Sold / Intake** — sold jobs in pre-construction
- **GHL Pipeline** — leads by stage (Appt Booked, Consultation, Proposal, Permitting)
- **Total on Map** — combined count of everything currently visible
